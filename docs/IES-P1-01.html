solutions

2022

  1. The indirect utility function is

    \(V=\frac{M^2}{4p_1 p_2}\)

    \(\frac{\partial V}{\partial M}= \frac{2M}{4p_1 p_2}\)

    and

    \(\frac{\partial V}{\partial p_1}= - \frac{M^2}{4p_2 p_1 ^2}\)

    from Roy’s identity we get that

    \(x_1^* = - \frac{\frac{\partial V}{\partial p_1}}{\frac{\partial V}{\partial M}}=-(\frac{- \frac{M^2}{4p_2 p_1 ^2}}{\frac{2M}{4p_1 p_2}})\)

    \(\implies x_1^* = \frac{M}{2p_1}\)

    Likewise

    \(x_2^*= - \frac{\frac{\partial V}{\partial p_2}}{\frac{\partial V}{\partial M}}=-(\frac{- \frac{M^2}{4p_1 p_2 ^2}}{\frac{2M}{4p_1 p_2}})\)

    \(\implies x_2^* = \frac {M}{2p_2}\)
